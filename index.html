Heads up before you visit

We're cash only.

No credit, no debit, no Apple Pay — just cash, the way diners used to be. It keeps prices low and the line moving.

  • An ATM is available inside Stop & Shop, across the parking lot.
  • A Wells Fargo ATM is in the plaza too.
  • Need a bigger withdrawal? There's a Bank of America branch a short drive away.

We're tucked back in there

How to Find Us in the Old Saybrook Shopping Center

We sit behind the bigger stores along Elm Street — it can be hard to spot the first time, so here's a map with the route highlighted.

Step by step

  1. Turn onto Elm Street from Route 1 (Boston Post Rd).
  2. Take the second right into the shopping center.
  3. Head to the right-hand corner of the lot, on the backside of HomeGoods. Look for the flag pole, our navy awnings, and the red Courtyard sign.
  4. Park anywhere out front and come on in.
